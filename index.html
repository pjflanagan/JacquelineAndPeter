Our story

A chance encounter

Jackie met Peter while she was on vacation in New York. Free of plans one evening, Jackie's friends suggested she try a dating app to kill time. Having exhausted his monthly swipes on Tinder, Peter gave a new app a try. The two downloaded Bounce, and went out that same evening. The couple went on two dates during Jackie's vacation and kept in touch after she left. Bounce went on to be a commercial failure, and was removed from the app store a year later.

Distance makes the heart grow fonder

Over the next two years, Jackie and Peter flew back and forth to see each other. A third date in Paris, a fourth in London, a fifth in Denver.

Caught a break

In 2020, the pair was fortunate enough to take advantage of working remote. Peter lived with Jackie in London for four months, and in Taipei for two more. Peter left his job before they got wise.

Our wedding

In June 2022, Jackie and Peter eloped! The couple went to City Hall and had a small celebration in a nearby bar in Chinatown.
