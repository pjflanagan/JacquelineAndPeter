Our Story

A chance encounter

Jackie and Peter met while Jackie was on vacation in New York. Overcome with boredom, Jackie went on a dating app to kill time one evening. Peter, having found no luck on the usual apps, was giving Bounce a try for the first time. The couple went on two dates during her vacation, Bounce went on to be a commercial failure, and was removed from the app store a year and a half later.

Distance makes the heart grow fonder

Over the next two years, Jackie and Peter flew back and forth to see each other. A third date in Paris, a fourth in London, a fifth in Denver.

Caught a break during COVID

During COVID we were fortunate enough to take advantage of working remote. Peter lived with Jackie in London for four months, and in Taipei for two more. Peter left his job before they got wise.

Our life together

A year and a half ago Jackie and Peter eloped!
